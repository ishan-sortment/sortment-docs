# Whatsapp

Sortment enables you to send WhatsApp messages by integrating with **official WhatsApp providers**, while keeping campaign creation, analytics, and delivery logs centralized.

***

### What This Section Covers

The WhatsApp section in Sortment documentation helps you:

* Understand **how WhatsApp works in Sortment**
* Identify **which WhatsApp setup you already have**
* Choose the **right integration guide**
* Configure senders, templates, and delivery tracking

This section does **not** sell or provision WhatsApp accounts—it explains how to **connect existing setups**.



### Things You Should Know Before Integrating WhatsApp

#### 1. WhatsApp Requires Templates

* All outbound WhatsApp messages use **pre-approved templates**
* Templates are approved by Meta (directly or via BSPs)
* Sortment can **sync or create templates**, depending on your provider

***

#### 2. Delivery Status Depends on Provider

* Some providers send delivery statuses automatically
* Others require **manual webhook / callback setup**
* Sortment logs all delivery events once configured

***

#### 3. Message Limits Are Enforced by Meta

* New WhatsApp accounts typically start at **1,000 messages/day**
* Limits increase based on **engagement and quality**
* Spam reports or blocks can reduce limits
