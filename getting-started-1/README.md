# Getting started

Sortment makes it easy to activate your AI-powered audiences and traits by syncing them directly from your data warehouse into the customer engagement platforms (CEPs) you already use.

We currently support direct integrations with leading CEPs including Iterable, Klaviyo, MoEngage, Marketo, Salesforce Marketing Cloud, and Responsys, allowing you to transfer any audience or trait created in Sortment straight into the project of your choice within these platforms.

#### Follow the setup guide below for your engagement platform

* [Iterable Integration](iterable.md)
* [Klaviyo Integration](klaviyo.md)
* [MoEngage Integration](moengage.md)
* [Marekto Integration](marketo.md)
* [SFMC Integration](salesforce-marketing-cloud.md)
* [Responsys Integration](responsys.md)



More integrations are on the way. If your team uses a different CEP, reach out to us at [support@sortment.com](mailto:support@sortment.com) and we’ll take it up.
